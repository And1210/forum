MECH 420

3.5 credits
Average Course Rating

Instructor: Ronald Anderson

Prerequisites

MECH 328 or ENPH 211 (PHYS 211) and ENPH 225 (PHYS 225) and registered in a BSCE or BASC Academic Program.

Course Description
